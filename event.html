Event Overview

Dated: 11 December 2025
Place: Lakshmi Shiksha Niketan, Danya, Almora

Himalaya Darshan Social Service Organization organized a motivational lecture for students of Classes 6 to 12, focusing on personal growth, improving concentration, understanding the true purpose of education, and recognizing individual responsibilities toward culture and society. The session aimed to inspire students to build a positive mindset, develop discipline, and contribute meaningfully to their community.

The session encouraged students to understand how to improve their lives, develop focus, set meaningful goals, and contribute positively to the nation and community.
